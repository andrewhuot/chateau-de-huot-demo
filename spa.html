Château Spa

3,000 sqm of pure restoration

Thermal pools, ancient stone hammam, and a full ritual menu drawing on the healing botanicals of Provence.

The Philosophy

A space apart

The Château Spa was conceived as a complete departure from the world beyond our gates. Drawing from a private underground thermal spring, the waters that fill our pools carry centuries of mineral wisdom — warm, restorative, and utterly unlike anything you will encounter elsewhere.

Every treatment is built around the ingredients that grow within reach of our walls: hand-harvested lavender from the estate's own fields, wild rosemary from the hillsides above Gordes, thyme gathered at dawn from the garrigue. These are not ingredients chosen for their romance — they are chosen because they work.

The spa is reserved exclusively for hotel guests, ensuring an atmosphere of genuine intimacy and calm that no day-visit property can replicate.

Facilities

Every element of restoration

Thermal Pools

Indoor and outdoor pools fed by our private 34°C mineral spring. The water moves — never stagnant, always alive.

Stone Hammam

Traditional hammam hewn from 14th-century stone, infused with eucalyptus steam. An ancient practice in an ancient space.

Himalayan Salt Room

Pink salt walls with programmable chromotherapy lighting. Each 45-minute session yields the respiratory equivalent of three days by the sea.

Flotation Pool

Supersaturated salt water in complete silence and near-darkness. A 60-minute float equals eight hours of deep sleep for many guests.

Fitness Studio

Full Technogym equipment suite with natural light over the lavender fields. Personal training sessions available on request.

Relaxation Lounges

Three firelit lounges with cashmere throws, herbal tea service, and views of the estate. Stay as long as you wish.
