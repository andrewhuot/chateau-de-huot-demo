Alfresco Elegance

Weddings

The château wedding

There are venues, and there is Château de Huot. The ancient stone of the main house, the scent of lavender carried on a warm Provençal breeze, the last light falling golden across the Luberon — no set designer could create what nature and four centuries of careful stewardship have built here.

We host a strictly limited number of weddings each year, ensuring that every couple receives the full, undivided attention of our team and the exclusive use of spaces that feel truly their own.

Ceremony Locations

The olive grove, the terrace, La Grande Salle, or the private chapel — four settings, four entirely different moods.

Bridal Preparation

A dedicated bridal suite with private bathroom, full-length mirrors, and natural light — available from dawn.

Estate Buyout

For full weekend weddings, exclusive use of all 24 suites and every venue on the estate.

Menu by Chef Laurent

A bespoke tasting menu designed around your story, your flavours, your wedding.
