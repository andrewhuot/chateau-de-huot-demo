Getting Here

Three ways to arrive

However you choose to travel, we will have a member of the concierge team ready to greet you on arrival.

By Air

Marseille Provence Airport — 1 hour 30 minutes by private car. The nearest major gateway, with direct connections from London, Paris, and major European cities.

Nice Côte d'Azur Airport — 2 hours by private car. Excellent intercontinental connections.

Private helicopter landing pad on site

By Train

Avignon TGV — 45 minutes by private car. Our preferred arrival point, combining the romance of rail with swift access to the estate.

Paris Gare de Lyon to Avignon — 2 hours 40 minutes by TGV direct. Arguably the most civilised way to begin a Provençal journey.

Private transfers available — please enquire

When to visit

May and June for lavender beginning to bloom, comfortable temperatures, and the estate at its most vivid. September and October for the vendange — the grape harvest — truffles, and the golden light of the Provençal autumn.

The estate is open year-round. Winter brings a quieter, more intimate atmosphere — the spa is especially beautiful in the low light of January, and truffle season peaks through February. In any season, our concierge team curates experiences suited to what Provence offers at that moment.
