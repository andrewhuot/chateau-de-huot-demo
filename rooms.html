Every Stay

Included with every stay

At Château de Huot, our standards begin where others end. These amenities are not extras — they are the foundation of your experience.

Thermal Pool Access

Complimentary access to our heated mineral pool and hydrotherapy circuit, open from 6 am to 10 pm.

Daily Housekeeping

Twice-daily service with fresh Provençal botanicals restocked and linens turned at your preference.

24-Hour Concierge

Your named concierge is reachable around the clock for reservations, arrangements, and the unexpected.

Estate Breakfast

A lavish Provençal breakfast served in the orangerie or delivered to your terrace — whichever suits your morning.

WiFi & Streaming

High-speed fibre throughout the estate, with a curated film and music library available on all room screens.

Bicycle Hire

Handcrafted touring bicycles for exploring the lavender routes and hill villages of the Luberon valley.

Provençal Bath Products

Exclusive formulations by Mas de la Rose — lavender, thyme, and almond oils harvested from our estate gardens.

Nightly Turndown

Fresh flowers, a handwritten note from your concierge, and a selection of local confiseries placed bedside each evening.
