The Seasons

Every visit is entirely different

Summer · June–August

Lavender in full bloom

Long golden evenings on the terrace. Private yacht charters along the Côte d'Azur. The perfume of 40,000 lavender plants drifting through your open windows.

Autumn · September–November

The harvest season

Truffle hunting at dawn. Private vineyard tours and blending sessions with our sommelier. Chef Renard's harvest tasting menu at La Vérrière.

Winter · December–February

The art of restoration

The spa is at its most intimate in winter. Firelit evenings, deep-tissue treatments, and the peaceful solitude of the estate without the crowds of high summer.
